Stages of Reading

The stages of reading development refer to the sequential progression of skills and abilities a person acquires as they learn to read. These stages typically involve a gradual shift from basic awareness of print to fluent and independent reading comprehension. It is basically a framework, describing the evolving relationship between the reader and the text as they move from initial exposure to proficient literacy.
